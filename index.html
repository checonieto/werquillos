Our Story

Family Tradition Since 2015

Los Werquillos is a family-owned Mexican food truck bringing authentic, traditional flavors to the Big Horn Basin, Wyoming area. We started our business with a simple mission: to share the genuine taste of Mexican cuisine with our community.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Every dish is prepared with care and love, just like we do for our own family.

We take pride in serving delicious tacos, burritos, quesadillas, and authentic Mexican snacks that remind our customers of home-cooked meals. Whether you're craving street-style tacos or hearty plates, we've got something for everyone.

Find us around Big Horn Basin and experience the taste of real Mexican food!
